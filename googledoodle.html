Steampunk Jubilee Day

Project name: Steampunk Jubilee Google Doodle

Summary: A google doodle animation that explores the possibilities of technology and reality in an alternate 19th century atmosophere. The Steampunk Jubilee celebrates the innovations of the steampunk universe from airships, fashion, and steam-powered cities.

Client: Google

Project Date: March 2018

Design Tools: Codepen, Github
