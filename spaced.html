Routes App

Project name: SPACED App

Big Idea: A mobile app for a traveling company that allows you to book a flight to space back, safely. Choose your otherwordly destination, the activities you want to do, and book your flight! Travelers also have the option to purchase additional space suit gear, for an even greater experience.

Client: Dan Petty

Project Date: April - June 2018

Responsibilities: Research, Prototyping, & Testing.

Platforms: Mobile Application

Design Tools: Sketch, InvisionApp

UX Methods : Sketches, Card Sorting, and Usability Testing.
